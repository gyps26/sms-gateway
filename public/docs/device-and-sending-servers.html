Device and Sending Servers

Device or Sending Server is responsible for sending the campaign. Each campaign can have multiple of those.

Status

The campaign is designed to monitor the progress of each SIM and Sender ID used in it. SIMs are associated with a Device, while Sender IDs are linked to a Sending Server, which handles the actual execution of tasks. The system tracks the status of the campaign on each Device and Sending Server. Detailed explanations of these statuses for Devices and Sending Servers involved in the campaign can be found below.

Pending

Initial status of the campaign on a Device or Sending Server. This means a campaign is not yet queued for processing.

Queued

It indicates that the campaign is queued for processing on a Device or Sending Server.

Stalled

It indicates that the campaign is postponed for the time being due to one of the following reasons.

  • Out of Quota set for SIMs or Sending Server. This applies solely to the message campaign.

  • The current time is not in Active Hours

  • The current day is not in Days of Week.

It will resume sending when all the above conditions are not violated.

Succeeded

It indicates that the campaign successfully finished sending on a Device or Sending Server. This doesn't mean that all messages or USSD pulls were sent successfully. It can still have some or all messages or USSD pulls in Failed status. It means that the Device or Sending Server executed its part of the campaign successfully, and it is now up to the operator or third party provider to send them further.

Failed

It indicates that the campaign failed. This can happen mostly likely due to lack of credits, connection issues with server or server error.

Cancelling

It indicates that the campaign is marked for cancellation on a Device or Sending Server.

Cancelled

It indicates that the campaign was canceled successfully on a Device or Sending Server.
